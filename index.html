Answers

1. Two ways to move a div around on the page

We can move a div in a variety of ways, two of them are:

  • we can add margins to the div to space it from the element that precedes it in the flow of the webpage
  • we can set a position of relative to set the div position relative to itself and then add the properties top,bottom,left and right to move it.

2. Stick a div onto the bottom or top of the page

To stick a div to the bottom or top of the page, we will set the css property position to fixed and add either a top or bottom of 0.
